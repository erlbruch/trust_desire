Instructions

This is a trust game with 2 players.

To start, participant A receives {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}; participant B receives nothing. Participant A can send some or all of his {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} to participant B. A can state an desired amount which will communicated to B. The desired amount has to be smaller than than the amount sent. Before B receives this amount (and the desired amount info) it will be tripled. Once B receives the tripled amount he can decide to send some or all of it back to A.
